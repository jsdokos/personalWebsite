Home

We at CatTails partner with local volunteers and fosters in order to provide a loving in caring enviornment for cats while they look for their forever home. Since we keep cats in homes, and not cages, we are able to see their true personality and help you find the cat that fits you and your life the best. We are "changing the world one cat at a time".

As another part of our mission to help provide cats with a safe and healthy life we focus on 4 values.
  • Commitment
  • Compassion
  • Respect
  • Responsibility
